How to Download Blackboard Videos

For some videos, you can simply right-click on the video and save it. For videos that support closed captions, when you right-click you'll see something about JWPlayer instead.

To download these videos, first make sure your bookmarks toolbar is visible. If you're not sure how to do this, check the links below.

  • For Chrome
  • For Firefox

Next, drag-and-drop this link up to your bookmarks toolbar: Download Blackboard Video

You should see a new button on your bookmarks toolbar that says "Download Blackboard Video." Now, on Blackboard, navigate to the video you'd like to download, click that new button, and you should arrive at a page that only has the video on it. You can now right-click on the video and save it.
